DONORS is a Non Profitable
Organization.

We focus our efforts on making, sure enough blood is available for patients when and where they need it.

According to WHO “world health organization”, many developing countries face severe shortage of blood. So the need for blood is
universal, but access to blood for all those who need it is not.

Voluntary and unpaid regular blood donation is an act of solidarity and is required all over the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect blood donors with recipients, save and improve lives every day.

Our vision

Make everyone aware of the need to donate blood so that there is no shortage in the blood bank.

Our Core Values

Our core values have shaped the culture of our organization from the very beginning and they still hold true today. Along with our organizational strategy, they act as our guide, our daily reference, ensuring that we speak and act consistently across our business at all times.
