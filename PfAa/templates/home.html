Who We Are?

Our mission is to connect blood donors with recipients, save and improve lives every day. We focus our efforts on making, sure enough blood is available for patients when and where they need it.

According to WHO “world health organization”, many developing countries face severe shortage of blood. So the need for blood is universal, but access to blood for all those who need it is not.

Voluntary and unpaid regular blood donation is an act of solidarity and is required all over the world.

THE PROBLEM

Many people around us struggle to find blood donors for critical patients, many people are willing to donate blood but finding an easy platform to use and to reach the seekers is a big task. and also many people do not want to donate blood thinking that these centers and organizations are selling it to patients.

THE GOAL

Our goal is to connect donors with recipients to ensure their blood goes directly to patients, Show the closest blood donors with their contact details so patients have easy access and communicate directly with them.
